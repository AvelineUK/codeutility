About Epoch Time

Unix epoch time (Unix timestamp) is the number of seconds since January 1, 1970 00:00:00 UTC. This calculator helps you:

  • Calculate future or past timestamps
  • Add time for expiration dates
  • Subtract time for historical dates
  • Convert between timestamp and human-readable formats
