About QR Codes

QR codes (Quick Response codes) can store various types of data and are commonly used for:

  • Website URLs and landing pages
  • Contact information (vCards)
  • Wi-Fi network credentials
  • Product information and tracking
  • Payment information
  • Event tickets and check-ins

Tip: Higher error correction levels make QR codes more resistant to damage but increase their complexity.
