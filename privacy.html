Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 3 December 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At CodeUtility, we take your privacy seriously. Our tools are designed to run entirely in your browser, which means your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal data or the content you process using our tools.

Data Processing

Client-Side Processing: All our free tools run entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • No data you enter into our tools is sent to our servers
  • No files you upload are transmitted over the internet
  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • Your data never leaves your computer

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or track any personal information or user data.

We use Netlify Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, such as page views, browser type, and general location. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected, and the data is used only to improve site performance and user experience. For more information, see Netlify's Privacy Policy.

Server Logs

Our hosting provider (Netlify) may collect basic server logs for infrastructure purposes (e.g., IP addresses, timestamps). These logs are managed by Netlify according to their privacy policy and are not accessible to us for tracking purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our tools may load JavaScript libraries from third-party CDN providers. These CDN providers may collect basic access logs. We have no control over their data collection practices.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Since most of our tools run entirely in your browser, the primary security responsibility lies with your device and browser.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. You can use our tools completely anonymously without any account or registration.

Children's Privacy

Our services do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
